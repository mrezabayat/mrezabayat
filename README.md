### 🧠 About Me

I’m a performance-focused **C++ engineer** passionate about **video compression**, **low-latency streaming**, and **immersive XR systems**.  
Currently at **V-Nova**, I work at the intersection of **research-grade codecs** and **production systems**, turning complex multimedia research into scalable, real-world products.

My goal: **Enable next-gen, real-time media experiences across platforms** — from embedded to cloud to XR.

### 🚀 Featured Projects & Achievements

#### 🎥 V-Nova Ltd (London, UK)
- **LCEVC + FFmpeg Low-Latency Streaming** — Solved pipeline bottlenecks for ultra-low latency delivery.  
- **XR Streaming to Apple Vision Pro** — Streamed 6DoF immersive video from CloudXR.  
- **Codec Integration** — Integrated AV1/VVC + LCEVC into VLC-iOS; enabled VC-6 decoding in AI/ML frameworks.  
- **Performance Optimization** — Reduced LCEVC decoding latency by **82%** on Linux/macOS.  
- **Custom iOS HLS Player** — Designed adaptive playback parser tuned for XR scenarios.  
- **Tooling & CI/CD** — Built pipelines for benchmarking, metric automation, and Apple CI systems.

#### ⚙️ Siemens (London, UK)
- Developed and maintained **State Estimation** systems for critical components.  
- Researched and prototyped **Hybrid Neural Networks** with C++/UML design.  
- Drove quality via **GoogleTest unit/integration testing** and **ticket triage** processes.  
- Led knowledge-sharing C++ sessions company-wide.

#### 🧩 Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (Barcelona, Spain)
- Contributed to an **EU research project** building a **C++ HPC library** for flexoelectric metamaterials.  
- Implemented **trimmed isogeometric analysis** in parallel HPC environments.  
- Modeled **multiphysics systems** with FEM and molecular dynamics.

#### ⚡ MAPNA Generator Engineering & Manufacturing Co.
- Collaborated with 40+ engineers on **heavy-duty generator design** using ANSYS, CATIA, and FEA.  
- Automated analysis workflows with **ANSYS ACT** and developed **parametric CAD models**.
